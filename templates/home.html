How It Works

Step 1: Train the Model

Initialize the system by training the face recognition model with student and teacher profiles at the start.

Step 2: Capture Group Photo

Take a group photo at the beginning of each class period to record attendance.

Step 3: Automatic Attendance

Upload the group photo, and the system automatically identifies and marks attendance for all individuals.

Our Features

MTCNN Face Detection

Utilizes MTCNN for precise face detection, ensuring accurate identification in group photos even in challenging conditions.

InceptionResNetV1 Recognition

Employs InceptionResNetV1 for robust face recognition, delivering high accuracy in matching faces to profiles.

PyTorch Framework

Built on PyTorch, enabling efficient deep learning model training and deployment for seamless performance.

Twilio Parental Notification

Automatically sends SMS notifications to parents when their child is absent, keeping them informed in real-time.

Automated Attendance Marking

Uses advanced face recognition to quickly and accurately mark attendance from group photos.

Detailed Attendance Dashboards

Provides intuitive dashboards for students and teachers to track attendance trends and statistics.
